credibility


  • credible: > 0.5
  • undefined: > 0.25 AND < 0.5
  • not credible: < 0.25
The credibility indicator shows you how credible the article you are reading is.
It is computed from the article itself and hyperlinks in the article text with the same domain. The entities of both the article and the hyperlink articles are being compared:
  • High conformity of the entities leads to high credibility.
  • Low conformity of the entities leads to low credibility
